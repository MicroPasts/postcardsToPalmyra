Transcription of the Palmyra Arch postcards

This project is an application for the crowd-sourcing of data from postcards collected by Zena Kemash (Royal Holloway) during the display of the replica of the arch of Palmyra, Trafalgar Square (April 2016). Transcribing these cards is very simple!

Please just transcribe what you see on the card! If you have extra comments, or the text is in a different language, please tell us!
