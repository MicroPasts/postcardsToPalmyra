Transcription of the Palmyra Arch postcards

This project is an application for the crowd-sourcing of data from postcards collected by Zena Kamash (Royal Holloway) during the display of the replica of the arch of Palmyra, Trafalgar Square (April 2016).

Transcribing these cards is very simple.

  • Transcribe the text on the card (if you can read it)
  • If the text is not in English, please check the language box
  • If you have comments on the postcard, please fill in the comments box

We are very grateful for your help.
